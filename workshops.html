Having taught workshops since 2018, learn calligraphy with me using teaching materials I've finessed over the years.

Private workshops

Private workshops are great for team bonding events, party organizers looking for a low-key activity, and anyone who wants more focused attention for a small group of students.

Workshops typically run about 2 hours for up to 20 students. I provide all materials that students can then take home for additional practice. Contact me with your event details and for pricing!

Public workshops

At the moment, I am not teaching public workshops. However, if you have a space you would like to offer regular calligraphy workshops in, feel free to reach out and see if we're a good fit!
