I'm Margaret, a New York-based calligrapher and designer.

When I was in grade school, my father taught me brush calligraphy, and since then I was hooked. I experimented with different lettering styles and calligraphy scripts, practicing on various mediums and with new tools (often perusing stationery stores when traveling).

Having worked as an art director, product designer, and calligrapher, my background has centered around understanding creative prompts and delivering beautiful finished pieces for clients.

My favorite thing about calligraphy is still how the simple act of writing letters by hand never fails to amaze an audience. Who knew learning cursive could be so rewarding?
