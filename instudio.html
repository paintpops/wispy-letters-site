How does it work?

What is your turnaround time? +

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity of the project, number of items that need to be calligraphed, and/or current workload. Most projects take 2-4 weeks, while smaller orders can generally be done in a week. Rush orders are available for an additional fee.

Do you provide the materials or do I send mine? +

Either option is available. Most clients either mail the materials, deliver via messenger, or drop it off themselves at my Long Island studio. I can also source the materials for a fee if needed.

For handwritten items, I recommend sending 10% extra quantities of materials to account for any ink testing or potential typos.

How many rounds of revisions are included? +

For branding, design, and spot calligraphy, I offer up to 3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are available for a fee.
